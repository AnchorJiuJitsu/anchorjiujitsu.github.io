Coach Mike Rock

Mike Rock is a Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Brown Belt. His Brazilian Jiu Jitsu journey began in 2010 and he quickly developed a passion for the art. Training in the art brings him joy, gratitude and confidence. The experience of coaching, mentoring youth and being a part of other individuals Jiu Jitsu journey is a truly amazing experience for him.
